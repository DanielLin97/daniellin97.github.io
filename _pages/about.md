Before, I obtained both of my B.E. and M.E. degree from PRIS Lab of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2019 and 2022 respectively, advised by [Prof. Guang Chen](https://x.com/fly51fly). Early on I was working as a research intern in Tencent (with AI Lab).
