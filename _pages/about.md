☀️About Me
======
This is Hongzhan Lin (林鸿展 in Chinese). I am a research fellow in [CTIC](https://ctic.nus.edu.sg) research centre at National University of Singapore (NUS), working closely with [Prof. Mong-Li Lee](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~leeml/) (Director of CTIC), [Prof. Wynne Hsu](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~whsu/) (Director of IDS), and [Prof. Tat-Seng Chua](https://www.chuatatseng.com/) (Director of NExT++). Previously, I obtained my PhD in NLP group at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) in 2026, advised by [Prof. Jing Ma](https://majingcuhk.github.io/). I was a visiting PhD student in [NExT++](https://www.nextcenter.org) research centre at NUS, working under the guidance of [Prof. Tat-Seng Chua](https://www.chuatatseng.com/) from 2024 to 2025. Before, I obtained both of my Bachelor and Master degrees from PRIS lab at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2019 and 2022 respectively, advised by [Prof. Guang Chen](https://x.com/fly51fly). Early on I was working as an NLP research intern in Tencent AI Lab.

My research interests lie in Natural Language Processing, Multimodal Reasoning, and Social Computing, etc. I am open to possible collaborations. <p class="red-notice">
📢 I am now looking for incoming master or bachelor interns at NUS SoC to finish their projects at CTIC. Should you be interested in visiting opportunities if you are a PhD student, please feel free to contact me.
</p> For those who are interested in working with me, please feel free to email me. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
